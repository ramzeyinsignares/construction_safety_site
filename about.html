Mission Statement

The mission of this project is to promote greater awareness of construction worker safety and to highlight practical, research-supported strategies for reducing risk on job sites. By drawing attention to both technological innovations and policy opportunities, this advocacy work seeks to encourage informed conversations among community members, project owners, and decision-makers.

At its core, this project is grounded in the belief that the people who build our homes, schools, and infrastructure deserve workplaces that prioritize their well-being. Safer construction is not only achievable—it is essential to a more just and sustainable future.
